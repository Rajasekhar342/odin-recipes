Masala Dosa

Masala dosa is a variation of the popular South Indian dosa, which has its origins in Tuluva Udupi cuisine of Karnataka.

It is popular in South India, it can be found in all other parts of the country and overseas.

There are variations in Masala dosa like Mysore masala dosa, Rava masala dosa, Onion masala dosa, Paper masala dosa, Cheese masala dosa etc..

Ingredients to make Masala Dosa

  • rice
  • lentils
  • ghee
  • fenugreek
  • curry leaves

Steps to make Masala Dosa

  1. Soaking rice and lentils overnight in water and then grinding it to a batter.
  2. after that the batter will be poured on a pan to make a thin cake
  3. while frying we can add stuffing and flavours we like
